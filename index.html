Common Inquiries

Clarifications

What guarantees are offered on materials craftsmanship?

All ShopTuch items carry a lifetime warranty covering structural degradation, manufacturing defects, and materials failures. All titanium and anodized aluminum items are certified corrosion-free.

How does your carbon-neutral program function?

We invest 1.5% of gross revenues directly into carbon direct-air extraction projects. All premium shipping options are calculated to maintain 100% net carbon neutrality.

Do you offer global expedited air cargo services?

Yes. Express Air Freight delivers to metropolitan centers within 24 to 48 hours of secure digital placement, complete with secure digital signature verifications.

Can I alter or reverse custom allocations?

Because items are allocated dynamically inside our limited editions, order modification parameters lock within 30 minutes of secure validation.
