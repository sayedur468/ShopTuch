ATELIER INTELLECT

Frequently Answered Queries

HOW CAN I REQUEST A PRIVATE COMMISSION?

To request a private custom commission, simply initiate contact through our specialized inquiry portal in the contact section or contact your personal Concierge directly via phone. Private design consultations are currently scheduled in Paris, London, Riyadh, and New York.

WHAT COUNTRIES DO YOU SHIP TO?

We provide complimentary prioritized secure shipping to 42 selected sovereign nations. All customs, handling procedures, and insurance coverages are fully pre-arranged and managed directly by our designated import-export brokers.

HOW ARE YOUR JEWELRY DIAMONDS SOURCED?

Every single diamond, sapphire, and pearl utilized in ShopTuch masterpieces is selected by our certified gemologists. We strictly adhere to the Kimberley Process, ensuring complete conflict-free credentials and ethical transparency.

WHAT IS YOUR ATELIER RETURN POLICY?

Our standard return window remains open for 14 calendar days upon delivery of unmodified off-the-rack collection garments. Custom bespoke creations are tailored specifically to individual requirements and therefore cannot be returned or refunded once construction commences.
